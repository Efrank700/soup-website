Registration

  • Registration will be opened at SOME TIME on September 15, 2017. Teams will be allocated places in the tournament on first-come, first-serve basis. All teams that have pre-registered will have a spot for one team reserved, and this spot will be confirmed by registering.
  • Science Olympiad at Penn reserves the right to close registration once 50 teams have been registered.
  • Registration will close on December 15, 2017. Teams will not be allowed to register after that date, and teams who have not submitted payment yet will forfeit their spot in the tournament.
  • Teams who withdraw from the tournament before December 15, 2017 will be refunded their tournament fees. After this deadline, teams will not receive a refund upon withdrawing.
  • Registration fees will be $60 for one team, and $50 for an additional team.

The Competition

  • The Penn Invitational is Division C Tournament. All national events will be run in accordance with the National Science Olympiad rules and with clarifications found here.
  • Where applicable, all events will be run in accordance with the regional level rules (i.e. State and National topics will not be covered).
  • Teams are expected to follow general National Science Olympiad rules and policies, including the Science Olympiad Code of Ethics and the Science Olympiad Building Policy
  • Scoring of the Penn Invitational will be in accordance with the National Science Olympiad Scoring Policy. All scoring decisions will be made final at the conclusion of the awards ceremony.
